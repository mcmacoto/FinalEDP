FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

?

What are your operating hours?

We're open daily from 11:00 AM to 9:30 PM, Monday to Sunday. Whether it's a weekday lunch or a weekend dinner, we're ready to serve you.

?

Are your dishes Halal-friendly?

Most of our menu items are Halal-friendly. However, please note that some dishes may contain pork or pork-derived ingredients. We recommend informing our staff of any dietary preferences or restrictions when ordering.

?

Do you offer delivery services?

At the moment, we do not offer delivery. However, customers are welcome to place pickup orders if they prefer to enjoy our food at home.

?

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially on weekends or for large groups, to ensure seating availability.

?

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments (e.g., GCash). Contactless payments are also supported.

?

Do you accommodate private events or functions?

Yes! We have private dining rooms perfect for business functions, intimate celebrations, or exclusive tasting menus. Reach out via our contact page for arrangements.
