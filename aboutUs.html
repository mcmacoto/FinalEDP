Who are we?

At Bondi & Bourke, we offer a modern Australian dining experience led by our award-winning chef, Wade Watson. We take pride in serving a bespoke menu inspired by Australia's favorite dishes in a bright and welcoming space.

For more private occasions, our intimate dining rooms accommodate up to 20 guests with full multimedia support. Our signature degustation package delivers a memorable journey through appetizers, mains, desserts, and "the best roast in town," all paired with handpicked wines.

About us

Bondi & Bourke offers a modern Australian dining experience led by award-winning Chef Wade Watson. In a bright, welcoming space, we serve a bespoke menu inspired by Aussie favorites.

Our private dining rooms host up to 20 guests, ideal for intimate events or corporate functions. Guests can enjoy a signature degustation featuring appetizers, mains, desserts, and "the best roast in town," paired with fine wines.

Whether it's business or celebration, Bondi & Bourke is the perfect place to gather.
